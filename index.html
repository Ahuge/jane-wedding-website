How we met

We met at The Beach House for lunch, one arrived with a to do list and the other arrived with romance to do.
It took a walk around Harrison lake, for Jane to finally pop the question “is this a date?”

Cut to our first Valentine’s Day together where two old men appear on the doorstep asking for shelter from their raining ceiling.
Gary and Cosmo (the fluffy white dog) were here to stay.

Many travels later to a few favorites including a Kos, Greece, Prague, Czech Republic, Lisbon, Portugal and an engagement in Maui November 2019.
Our first wedding date was May 25, 2020, then it moved to October 3, 2020, then June 5, 2021 and now we are at September 25, 2021.

Covid 19 Protocol

All guests should be double vaccinated to attend our wedding.
In addition, please bring either a paper or electronic copy of your vaccination record and government photo I.D.
The Beach House will check this at the door before you are able to enter the restaurant.
Don’t forget your mask!

The reception is divided into two areas: an outdoor, covered patio where mingling is allowed and you can remove your mask if you feel comfortable.
Inside, masks must be worn at all times unless you are seated and eating.
Extra masks and hand sanitizer are available if you need them.
